About

A small company with a wide horizon.

Who we are

Cascade Alliance is an independent, privately held company based in Warsaw, Poland.

We build and operate a portfolio of ventures across brands, software, web, marketing, and hospitality. Some we create ourselves; others we grow in partnership. What ties them together is a way of working, not a single industry.

We stay deliberately small. It keeps us close to the work and free to move at our own pace — choosing the right ideas, then giving them the structure and patience to become something lasting.

What we value

Principles we build on.

01

Independent
